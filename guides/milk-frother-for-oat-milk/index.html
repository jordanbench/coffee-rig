How to choose without overbuying

Start with the constraint that will annoy you every week. For some buyers that is cleaning; for others it is space, noise, adjustment range, fit, or whether the product adds another routine. A better purchase is the one you will keep using after the first week.

When two products look similar, compare the support details: replacement parts, consumables, setup steps, storage, and whether the product can be cleaned or adjusted without special tools.
